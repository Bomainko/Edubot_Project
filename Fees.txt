**Question:** If you have any questions about your fee status, which are not answered below, or are having financial difficulties and need guidance, who should you contact?**Answer:** Get in touch with our [student advisers](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/current-students/support-and-facilities/student-advice).**Question:** If you have any additional queries about paying your fees, who should you contact?**Answer:** Contact your local [finance office](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/fees-and-funding/contact-us).**Question:** Who pays the Home rate of tuition fee?**Answer:** When you have a course offer, the Admissions Office will assess your fee status. They may ask you to complete a fee assessment form, which is usually sent to you with the course offer letter. If you're unsure about your fee status, get in touch with [student advice](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/current-students/support-and-facilities/student-advice).**Question:** Will my fee status remain the same for the duration of my studies?**Answer:** Your fee status is assessed before the start of the course and normally remains the same for the duration of the course. However, in certain circumstances, fee status can change part-way through a course.**Question:** Where can I find more details on fee statuses?**Answer:** Detailed information about the qualifying rules for Home fee status can be found on the [UKCISA website](https://www.ukcisa.org.uk/Information--Advice/Fees-and-Money/Home-or-Overseas-fees-the-basics). If you'd like to discuss your fee status or believe you may now qualify for Home fees, contact [student advice](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/current-students/support-and-facilities/student-advice).**Question:** How do I pay my fees and what payment methods are available?**Answer:** The easiest way is to [pay online](https://payonline.westminster.ac.uk/open). For full details on payment methods, visit our [Paying your fees page](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/fees-and-funding/fees/paying-your-fees).**Question:** How can I find out what I owe?**Answer:** To find out how much you owe, [log in to SRSWeb](https://auth.westminster.ac.uk/vpn/tmindex.html). You will also receive an invoice and statement to your University email address.**Question:** Can I pay my fee by instalments?**Answer:** Only certain students with a personal fee liability of over £2,000 will be able to pay by instalments. Visit our [Paying your fees page](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/fees-and-funding/fees/paying-your-fees) for full details.**Question:** When should I pay my fees?**Answer:** You can pay tuition fees for the year in full by the last day of enrolment. For those who qualify for instalments, refer to our [paying your fees page](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/study/fees-and-funding/fees/paying-your-fees).**Question:** Is there an alumni discount?**Answer:** The University of Westminster offers a 20% discount on its full- and part-time Masters courses and PhD courses for alumni in certain categories. See our [Alumni discounts page](https://www.westminster.ac.uk/about-us/alumni-and-supporters/alumni-benefits/alumni-discount) for more details.
